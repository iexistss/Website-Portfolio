About Me

I am Justin Otto Gitonga Mwandiki, born on 17th April 2010, and I am currently a Year 11 student at Rusinga Schools, Gitanga Road, Nairobi. My academic journey has shaped who I am today, starting at Riara School from 2011 to 2018, followed by Moi Educational Centre from 2018 to 2020, then Light International School from 2020 to 2023, and finally Rusinga Schools from 2023 to 2025. Each stage contributed to my growth both academically and personally, helping me become a disciplined, curious, and innovative student.

My journey in technology began when I was 8 years old, when I started coding from scratch. I attended Tinker Education and participated in numerous robotics classes and competitions, which built my foundation in electronics, problem-solving, and critical thinking. These experiences played a major role in shaping my passion for innovation.
